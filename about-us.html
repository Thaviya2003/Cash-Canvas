About Cash Canvas

Curating Comfort • Crafting Class

Our Philosophy

A Vision of
Atmospheric Wealth

At Cash Canvas, we believe that your home is the ultimate gallery of your life’s success. We didn't just start a decor brand; we started a movement to redefine Sri Lankan living spaces. My passion for design has always been rooted in the idea that luxury isn't just about what you own—it's about how your space makes you feel.

The inspiration for our signature "Money Art" came from a desire to visualize ambition. We wanted to create pieces that act as daily reminders of prosperity and drive, blending them with timeless, sophisticated aesthetics.

Our Promise to You: Every single piece in our collection is personally sourced by us. We ensure that every item is beautifully repackaged with the premium care it deserves and delivered securely to any corner of Sri Lanka. We treat every order as if it were for our own home.
